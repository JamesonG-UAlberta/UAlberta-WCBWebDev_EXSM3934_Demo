The Purpose of Responsive Design

Responsive design allows HTML pages and their designs to scale or completely reorganize to better suit different screen sizes. For example, on a mobile device, forms may be laid out in a single column, versus on a large desktop screen they may be in two or three columns. We can also change font sizes to be more readable, change menu styles into a collapsable format, and many other things to make a website mobile-friendly.
